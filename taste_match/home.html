About This Project

Finding your next go‑to food spot shouldn’t feel like a chore. Whether you’re planning a date, celebrating a milestone, or just grabbing a bite with friends, TasteMatch has you covered. TasteMatch is a web application built to serve the community by offering personalized recommendations based on your preferences, personality, and curiosity. Founded by four university students, our mission is simple: make discovering your next favorite place fast, fun, and effortless. We proudly spotlight small Chicagoland businesses alongside the restaurant brands you already love. Craving sushi, tacos, boba, coffee, falafel, or fine dining? We’ve got you. Need an impressive spot for a first date? We’ve got you. Want something quick and casual? Still got you. Wherever your appetite leads, TasteMatch helps you get there—quickly and confidently.

Why Use It?

TasteMatch takes the guesswork out of choosing where to eat. Instead of scrolling endlessly through reviews, menus, and maps, you get fast, personalized recommendations that actually match what you’re craving. Here’s what makes it worth using:

  • Personalized to you — Your preferences, personality, and curiosity shape every recommendation. No generic lists. No random picks.

  • Fast and effortless — Tell us what you’re in the mood for, and we’ll handle the rest.

  • Supports local businesses — We highlight small Chicagoland restaurants alongside the big names you already know.

  • Perfect for any occasion — First dates, late‑night cravings, quick bites, celebrations, or spontaneous adventures.

  • Discover hidden gems — Explore spots you might never find on your own, curated just for your taste.

  • TasteMatch makes finding your next food spot feel simple, fun, and surprisingly accurate. It’s your shortcut to great eats.

${"★".repeat(rating)}

${text}

`; demoReviews.prepend(card); demoRatings.push(rating); updateDemoAverage(); sortDemoReviews(); demoReviewForm.reset(); demoSelectedRating = 0; demoStars.forEach((s) => s.classList.remove("active")); }); demoSortSelect.addEventListener("change", sortDemoReviews); updateDemoAverage(); //matchmaker demo JS script
