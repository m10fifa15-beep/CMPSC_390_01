//Problem Statement
What problem does this solve?

Gives users a opportunity to give subjective assessments of locations to give other users an idea of what they can expect at said locations prior to their visit.

User Flow
Step by step from start to finish

Making a review system in the backend. Making it submit and retrieve information from the database.
Reviewing DB to make sure the reviews are being added and stored correctly.

Frontend changes
Backend routes

Files expected to be created or modified

Frontend page to let users leave reviews.
Adding a review table to store page to leave independent reviews.
